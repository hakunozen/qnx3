Our Legacy
  • Enabling Digital Technology

    2017

    Questronix Corporation is a systems integrator that provides IT solutions for various industries. This is premised on helping customers to increase competitive edge in terms of reliable information delivery. This turn assures its employees opportunities for professional growth and better family life. As these are sustained, the company's stakeholders are assured of fair return on their investments.

  • IT Systems Integrator

    2007

    Questronix Corporation recognized a huge IT market by focusing in the Open Systems Technology. In 1990, gearing up to industry competition, it was appointed by IBM as its first business partner for Rs/6000 (now known as System p). Wtihin years, the company emerged as on of IBM's leading business partners having been instrumental in making System p a dominant platform.

  • IBM Partner

    1997

    Cognizant of the growing need for a fast, reliable and efficient information delivery in the country's emerging IT market, A group of dynamic businessmen and professionals established Questronix Corporation in 1987.

  • Application Development

    1987

    Started in 1987, Questronix has proven to be one of the most trusted IT solution provider and systems integration in the Philippines. We continuously adopt to the rapid change of technology providing latest technology and services. Today, we are celebrating our 30 years in the IT industry.
