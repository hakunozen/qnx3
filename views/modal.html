Our Legacy
  • Enabling Digital Transformation

    2017

    On its 30th year, Questronix has sustained strong business relationships with its partners, earned the trust and support of top clients in their respective industries, and has garnered prestigious awards and accolades. With the emergence of digital transformation, Questronix constantly innovates, adapts to the changes in the technology and strives to assist their clients achieve their digital transformation goals. All these demonstrate why Questronix is widely known as one of the best IT solutions and service providers in the country.

  • Systems Integrator

    2000’s

    Questronix has grown to be a prominent player in the industry. It became the very first business partner in the Philippines to be awarded the exclusive Premier Business Partner status by IBM. Such an achievement was a recognition of Questronix’s determination to continuously develop and invest in the latest technologies, its people, and its commitment to working with its business partners.

  • IBM Business Partner

    1990’s

    IBM, recognizing Questronix’s skills and competency in the IT market, appointed it as the very first Business Partner for the RS/6000 (now known as System P). In just a few years, Questronix developed into one of IBM’s leading business partners having been instrumental in the dominance of System P as a platform.

  • The Inception

    1987

    It began as a data encoding company where a group of dynamic businessmen and professionals, cognizant of the growing need for a fast, reliable and efficient IT provider in the country’s emerging IT market, established Questronix Corporation in 1987.
